Background Information

Context

The final project for my AP US History class involved picking a decade and talking about it in the context of the USA of course. I selected the 1860s. The only requirement for the final project was to present the information to the class in any way we wanted to. I decided to make a video with images and jokes to convey the information. I was complimented for this video by a peer long after presenting this video.
