Context

During History class in 10th grade we were tasked with talking about a certain invention during the industrial revolution. In this video I used a style of dragging and dropping elements using mspaint. With the commentary and some visuals on the screen it can make the video out to be rather funny. The whole class, even the teacher, got some really good chuckles out of it.
